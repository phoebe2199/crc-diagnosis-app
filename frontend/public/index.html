ABOUT OUR PROJECT

Purpose

This tool has been made for clinicians for the purpose of colorectal cancer diagnosis and is intended to provide robust and immediate diagnosis.

Importance

Computer-adided diagnosis tools like ours have the potential to revolutionise the medical field, providing more efficient and effective diagnoses that can lead to better patient outcomes.

Provision

While our tool is currently in the proof-of-concept stage and not publicy available, we hope to make it more widely accessible in the future. We are continuing to refine the tool and improve its accessibility, with the ultimate goal of helping more people receive accurate diagnoses for colorectal cancer.

Technology

Our tool uses a type of artificial intelligence called convolutional neural networks (CNNs). We have trained these networks using different architectures, including VGG16, ResNet50, and InceptionV3, to classify the tissue class of a histopathology image stained with H&E. The CNN analyses the features of an image, such as colour and texture, to identify patterns that are indicative of cancerous tissue.
